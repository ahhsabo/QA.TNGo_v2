User manual
To use TapGo card, customers follow the following steps:
Step 1: Activate

Customers scan the QR code on the front of the card to send an activation message to "8066". The system will send a confirmation message of successful card activation to the activation phone number.

Step 2: Rent a bicycle

Tap the tag on the RFID symbol on the lock or the area to the right of the QR code on the lock to unlock. The lock will play a sound when the card is touched and automatically unlock for guests to rent the car.

Step 3: Return the bicycle

Park the car neatly at the station, close the car lock and touch the card on the RFID symbol on the lock within 30 seconds from the time the lock is closed to confirm the return of the car. If the customer touches the card after 30 seconds, the lock will open again.

Step 4: Deposit money

When the amount of money in the card is used up, customers can top up in 2 ways:

  • Top up via bank account: Open any mobile banking application, scan the QRcode on the back of your TapGo card and enter the amount to deposit to transfer.
  • Cash deposit (coming soon): You bring your TapGo card to the convenience store system for support to load cash into the card.
Step 5: Reuse

When you no longer need to use the card, it is recommended that you bring the card to pay at the card point of sale to reuse and receive a small gift from TNGo for your act of protecting the environment!
