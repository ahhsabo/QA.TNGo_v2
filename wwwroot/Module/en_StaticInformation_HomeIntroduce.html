About TNGo

Public bicycle service TNGo (TN-Go) brings a new form of urban transport that is civilized, convenient, healthy and environmentally friendly. With just a few simple steps on the mobile application, road users can easily rent, move and return the bike at any TNGo bike station in the city. TNGo will help connect other public transport systems such as buses, trains, etc., making urban mobility more flexible and convenient.

TNGo public bicycle service is now available in 05 provinces and cities: TP. Hồ Chí Minh, Vũng Tàu, Quy Nhơn, Hải Phòng, Đà Nẵng.
